ABOUT US

OUR STORY

We don’t do therapy. We
do chai. Same result.

It began in 2016 with a thought, “why isn’t great chai just around the corner, like coffee?” That thought turned into a spark. And that spark? It brewed Chai Kings.

What started in Chennai with one cozy outlet has grown into 50+ tea stops across Chennai, Hyderabad, and Coimbatore. Every day, lakhs of chai lovers sip, smile, and pass it on.

From morning office routines to late-night snack cravings, we’ve become part of the daily chai-graphy of people’s lives.





Here's Why?

The dream? Make chai that’s clean, consistent, and close by. Serve it with pride. Keep it pocket-friendly. Never compromise on the soul of it.

We never wanted to be a big chain with generic vibes. We wanted to build a space that feels local, personal, and full of flavor, always. A chai stop that feels like it knows your order before you say it.

If it’s hot, honest, and keeps your day going, it’s a Chai Kings cup.
