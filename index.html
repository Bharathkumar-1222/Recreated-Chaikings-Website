CHAI KINGS

We’re more than just chai.

In 2016, we started with a simple question — why isn’t great chai easily accessible?

From that spark, Chai Kings was born in Chennai, with a vision to redefine the way Chennai enjoys its most beloved beverage.

From a humble start, we’ve grown to over 60+ outlets across Chennai, Hyderabad, and Coimbatore, brewing lakhs of cups each month for chai lovers who search for the best Chai cafe, and land up at their neighbourhood Chai Kings.

Our mission? To make high-quality, hygienic, and affordable chai available to everyone — whether you’re sipping a hot cup of ginger chai, ordering a chai flask online, or ordering chai in bulk for you and your team to enjoy at work.
